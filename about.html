About Mike Bell

What’s good?

Hi, my name is Mike. I’m a lot of things, but to sum it up I guess you could call me a hacker.

I’ve been playing video games most of my life, starting with Super Mario Bros. on the Nintendo Entertainment System in 1989. I knew back then that I wanted to learn where this amazing thing came from and how to build things like it.

That led me to studying Computer Science and Japanese.

I’ve been writing code and studying computers since I was 10 years old, around 1996.
